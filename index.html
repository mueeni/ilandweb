About Us

Ma’din i Land is a new residential campus started for Boys Empowerment

Ma’din i Land is a new residential campus started in Vicennium period, the 20th celebrations of Ma’din Academy. C Ravindranadh, educational minister for Kerala launched this campus in last December. It is specially meant for boys.

The society longing for relevant interventions from religious propagators, as the every nook and corner of society fully fledged in all spheres, by the revolutionary advance of technology. We, ma’din i-Land residential institution calculates to nurture a school of propagators who will cease the thirst of oursociety. Today, we aims at making of ‘the leaders’ through the advanced syllabus including management skill developing, communicative skill developing. Our project is developed amidst of natural greenery, far from the knotty atmosphere of urbanisation. Tomorrow, there will be a school of outstanding leaders as a fruitful result of the project, as in chairman’s d reams , inshaallah..

  • Dialy Moral Training with School Education.
  • Mission challenges all to be peacemakers, changemakers.
  • Vibrant Community of Highly Engaged Students
